Sports Time!!

A human activity involving physical exertion and skill as the primary focus of the activity, with elements of competition or social participation where rules and patterns of behaviour governing the activity exist formally through organisations and is generally recognised as a sport.

Organised sport has many physical, developmental, psychological and social benefits for children. Playing sport helps children learn to control their emotions and channel negative feelings in a positive way. Playing sport helps children build resilience and feel better about themselves.

POWER LIFTING

Powerlifting can have many benefits, including:
Strength:
Powerlifting can help build strength and muscle mass through the three main lifts: the squat, bench press, and deadlift. These compound movements target many muscles throughout the body.
Bone health:
Strong muscles can lead to stronger bones, which can help decrease the risk of osteoporosis.
Other abilities:
Powerlifting can also help improve sprint speed and jump height.
Fat loss:
Increased muscle mass can lead to a higher basal metabolic rate (BMR), which can help with fat loss.
Mental health:
Powerlifting can be used as a coping mechanism to process difficult emotions and can improve your mood.
Social support:
The powerlifting community can be supportive, which can help with your fitness journey.

CYCLING

Cycling has many benefits, including health, environmental, and social benefits:
Health benefits:
Cycling is an aerobic activity that improves your cardiovascular fitness, muscle strength, flexibility, and joint mobility. It can also help you breathe deeper, sweat, and increase your body temperature, which can improve your overall fitness. Cycling can also help reduce stress, anxiety, and depression, and can help prevent or manage disease. Cycling can also help strengthen your bones and decrease body fat levels.
Environmental benefits:
Cycling is a pollution-free mode of transportation that uses minimal fossil fuels. It can help reduce noise pollution, air pollution, and greenhouse gas emissions. Cycling can also help conserve space in cities, which can lead to more plant life and less concrete.
Social benefits:
Cycling can help reduce traffic congestion in cities, which can reduce travel times for everyone. Cycling can also foster social interaction.

VOLLEYBALL

Volleyball is a popular sport that can be played by people of all ages and skill levels, both indoors and outdoors, and year-round. It can be played with a partner or as a team, and offers many benefits, including:

Physical health :
Volleyball can improve aerobic ability, hand-eye coordination, reflexes, balance, stamina, speed, and agility. It can also help build muscle and strength in the arms and legs, and burn calories. A 20-minute game of volleyball can use the same amount of energy as jogging a mile.
Mental health :
Volleyball can improve mental well-being, reduce stress, and boost mood. Playing as part of a team can also strengthen friendships and increase self-confidence.
Character-building skills:
Volleyball can help develop skills such as leadership, teamwork, respect, and communication.
